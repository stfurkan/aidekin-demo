About

Copperleaf started as a weekend bake stall at the Brightwater harbor market. The beans are roasted for us weekly by Alder Roasting Co., the bread is our own sourdough, and the menu changes with whatever the growers on Harbor Street have that week.

"Third place, first names." That's the whole business plan.
