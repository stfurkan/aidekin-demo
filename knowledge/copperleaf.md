# Copperleaf Café

Copperleaf Café is a neighborhood coffee shop and brunch spot on the corner of Alder Lane and Harbor Street, at 14 Alder Lane, Brightwater (established 2019). We pull roast-forward espresso, brew single-origin pour-overs, and serve a small seasonal brunch, with pastries baked in-house every morning. The menu prices below match what is posted in the café.

## Hours

What time does Copperleaf Café open and close, and what time do you close on Sunday? Monday to Friday we are open from 7:30 in the morning to 18:00 (6 in the evening). On Saturday and Sunday we are open from 8:00 in the morning to 17:00 (5 in the afternoon), so on Sunday we close at 17:00. The kitchen serves brunch until 14:30 (2:30 in the afternoon) every day; drinks and pastries are available until the café closes.

## Location and parking

Copperleaf Café is at 14 Alder Lane, Brightwater, on the corner of Alder Lane and Harbor Street. It is a 5 minute walk from Brightwater Central station. There is a parking lot directly behind the building, with the entrance on Harbor Street.

## Coffee and drinks

How much is a coffee, an espresso, a latte, a cappuccino, a cortado, or a flat white at Copperleaf? An espresso is 3.0, and our espresso drinks, including the cortado, flat white, latte, and cappuccino, are from 3.0. Batch brew filter coffee is 3.2, and a single-origin pour-over, which changes regularly, is 5.5, so ask about today's pour-over. Chai, matcha, and hot chocolate are 4.5 each. Oat, almond, and soy milk are available at no extra charge.

## Brunch menu

How much is brunch, and what does the shakshuka, avocado toast, or pancakes cost? The brunch menu is served daily until 14:30 (2:30 in the afternoon). Ricotta and honey sourdough toast is 9, avocado toast with pickled chili is 11, shakshuka with grilled sourdough is 13, buttermilk pancakes with fruit are 12, and the bacon and egg brioche roll is 10.

## From the oven

Pastries are baked in-house each morning. A croissant is 3.8, a cardamom bun 4.2, a seasonal galette 5, and our signature copperleaf cinnamon knot is 4.5. A pastry box of a dozen assorted pastries is 38, with 48 hours' notice.

## Payment methods

What payment methods do you accept, and can I pay by credit card or Apple Pay? We accept all major credit and debit cards, contactless, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash. There is no minimum spend for card or contactless payments, and you can tap to pay with your phone or watch at the counter.

## Dietary options and allergens

Gluten-free and vegan options are baked daily; there are always at least a couple of vegan pastries, and gluten-free bread is available for toast. The shakshuka and pancakes can be made vegetarian or vegan on request. Our kitchen handles nuts, gluten, dairy, and eggs, so we cannot guarantee zero cross-contact; please tell the team about any allergy when ordering and they will walk you through safe options.

## Wifi

Free wifi is available for customers. The password is printed at the bottom of your receipt, or you can ask at the counter.

## Reservations and groups

Copperleaf is walk-in first: tables are not reserved for small parties. For groups of six or more, email hello@copperleafcafe.com at least a day ahead and we will hold the big communal table where we can.

## Dogs and pets

Can I bring my dog to Copperleaf? Yes: dogs are welcome on the patio, and there are fresh water bowls out for them. Inside the café we welcome service animals. Other pets are happiest at home, but your dog is always a friend on the patio.

## Kids, takeaway, and restrooms

Is Copperleaf good for kids and families, do you have high chairs, and can I get my order to go? Children are welcome, and we keep a few high chairs by the window. Everything on the menu is available for takeaway, and we are always happy to fill a clean reusable cup. Restrooms, including an accessible restroom, are inside past the counter, and the café is step-free from the Alder Lane entrance.

## Catering and pastry boxes

We make pastry boxes of a dozen assorted pastries for 38, with at least 48 hours' notice, ordered by email at hello@copperleafcafe.com. For office catering or a larger event, mention the date and headcount in your email and we will get back to you.

## Beans, gift cards, and loyalty

We sell 250 gram bags of the beans we brew, roasted for us weekly by Alder Roasting Co.; ask at the counter. Gift cards are available at the counter in any amount. Our loyalty card gives you the tenth coffee free, one stamp per visit, and it never expires.

## Events

Copperleaf hosts a free open mic night on the first Friday evening of every month, from 19:00 to 21:00, with live music from local performers and a short evening menu from the kitchen.

## About

Copperleaf started as a weekend bake stall at the Brightwater harbor market. The beans are roasted for us weekly by Alder Roasting Co., the bread is our own sourdough, and the menu changes with whatever the growers on Harbor Street have that week. Our whole idea is simple: third place, first names.

## Contact

Email hello@copperleafcafe.com for group bookings, catering, lost and found, or feedback. The café does not take orders by email; drinks and brunch are ordered at the counter.
